Privacy Policy

Last updated on the 31st of January, 2025.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Núcleo de Estudantes de Informática da Associação Académica de Coimbra, also referred to as NEI/AAC, NEIAAC or neiaac in some contexts. While part of the Associação Académica de Coimbra (AAC), we operate with some degree of independence.

In this document, references to “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to NEI/AAC.

Your privacy is important to us, in the following sections we will explain how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our services.

What Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and contact information (e.g., email address, phone number, student ID)
  • Demographic details (e.g., age, gender, nationality, education level)
  • Website usage data (e.g., IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited)

Legal Basis for Processing Data

We process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you explicitly agree to the processing of your data.
  • Contract: When processing is necessary to fulfill a contract with you.
  • Legal Obligation: When we must comply with a legal requirement.
  • Legitimate Interest: When processing is necessary for the core functions of our services.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary. Once it is no longer required, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Restrict or object to the processing of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at geral@neiaac.com.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data but may share it with service providers for operational purposes only. These third parties include:

  • Payment processors (e.g., for event entry payments)
  • External account providers (e.g., Google for OAuth login)

International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers, such as Google, may process data outside the European Economic Area (EEA). When this occurs, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place, such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Cookie Policy

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance the user experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. This technology will never be used by us for tracking or advertising purposes.

Third-party services we use may place cookies on our website, according to their own terms and policies. These cookies may include tracking for advertising purposes depending on the service.

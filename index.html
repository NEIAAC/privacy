Privacy Policy

Last updated on the 2nd of february, 2025.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Núcleo de Estudantes de Informática da Associação Académica de Coimbra, also referred to as NEI/AAC, NEIAAC or neiaac in some contexts. While part of the Associação Académica de Coimbra (AAC), we operate with some degree of independence.

Hereinafter, references to we, us and our shall refer to NEI/AAC.

Please note that some provisions may only apply to certain categories of users. In the absence of any such mention, clauses apply to all users.

Your privacy is important to us. The following sections explain how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our services.

Scope and Applicability

This document applies to:

  • Members of NEI/AAC.
  • Users of our websites or other online platforms.
  • Event participants and workshop attendees, when organized with or by us.
  • Individuals who contact us via email or social media.

This policy does not cover third-party websites or services linked from our platforms. We encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

Data We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, student ID.
  • Demographic Details: Age, gender, nationality, education level.
  • Website Usage Data: IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, interaction logs.

Legal Basis for Processing Data

We process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you explicitly agree to the processing of your data.
  • Contract: When processing is necessary to fulfill a contract with you.
  • Legal Obligation: When we must comply with a legal requirement.
  • Legitimate Interest: When processing is necessary for the core functions of our services.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for the purposes outlined in this policy and only for as long as necessary. Once it is no longer required, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Restrict or object to the processing of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at geral@neiaac.com.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data but may share it with service providers for operational purposes only. These third parties include:

  • Payment Processors: to handle entry fees.
  • External Authentication Provideers: For OAuth-based logins (e.g., Google).

We always guarantee that our third-party service providers comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or equivalent data protection standards.

International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers, such as Google, may process data outside the European Economic Area (EEA). When this occurs, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place, such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure:

  • Encrypted storage of sensitive data.
  • Access controls and authentication protocols.

Cookie Policy

Our websites may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance the user experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. This technology will never be used by us for tracking or advertising purposes. Users can control cookie preferences through their browser settings or via a dedicated cookie management tool (if available on the website).

Third-party services we use may place cookies on our websites for their own purposes, these fall under their respective privacy policies.

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Assess the impact and take necessary action.
  • Notify affected users if their data is compromised.
  • Report the breach to the relevant data protection authorities when required.

Automated Decision-Making & Profiling

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling techniques.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our privacy pipeline or when required by law. We will notify users of any significant changes that impact them via email, through our websites or other communication channels.
